❓ FAQ
======

Q: Is the system production-ready?
A: Yes! All components are production-ready and fully functional.

Q: How do I add a new game?
A: See QUICK_START.js or README.md "How to Extend" section.
   Takes ~5 minutes.

Q: Can I change the colors?
A: Yes! Edit tailwind.config.js colors section.

Q: Can I change the language?
A: Yes! Edit utils/constants.js SINHALA_UI section.

Q: Is progress automatically saved?
A: Yes! localStorage automatically saves after level completion.

Q: What if localStorage is full?
A: It won't happen with our data size, but you can manually clear:
   localStorage.removeItem('wmProgressData');

Q: How do I test the level unlock system?
A: Use the useProgress hook directly:
   const { completeLevel, resetProgress } = useProgress();
